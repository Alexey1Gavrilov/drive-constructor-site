# Pump

Power on shaft of a pump can be calculated as

$$
	\Phi(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi}} \int_{-\infty}^x e^{-\xi^2/2}\; d\xi
$$


$$
	P = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi}}
$$
